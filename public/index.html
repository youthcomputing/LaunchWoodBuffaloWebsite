About the event

Launch Wood Buffalo is Fort McMurray's only annual innovation conference. The goal of the event is to give opportunities to aspiring entrepreneurs in the community to learn and network.

The event will include keynotes and panels where skilled professionals will teach the community how to grow and improve a business. Furthermore, attendees will be able to sit in on panels with successful entrepreneurs with the ability to ask any desired questions.

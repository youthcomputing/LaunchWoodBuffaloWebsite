About the event

Launch Wood Buffalo is Fort McMurray's only annual innovation conference including a community pitch competition. The goal of the event is to give oppurtunities to aspiring entrepreneurs in the community to learn and network.

The event will include 3 workshops, 2 keynotes, and one panel where skilled proffesionals will teach the community how to grow and improve a business. Furthermore, attendees will be able to sit in on panels with successful entrepreneurs with the ability to ask any desired questions. Lastly, 8 teams will pitch their business ideas to judges in the attempt of winning a grand prize.

8 Panelists

A panel filled with successful entrepreneurs and business people each. Feel free to ask any question that comes to mind!

3 Workshops

Join up to 3 workshops in which you can learn how to grow and improve a business. Workshops will range from product creation to starting from scratch.

2 Keynotes

Look inside the minds of the most successful entrepreneurs and innovators in Alberta!

Pitch Competition

Watch or compete in a pitch competition between 8 teams who are fighting for the grand prize. Teams will explain their business models to a set of judges who will decide the winner.
